Long Live WinGoku

WinGoku was started back in 2012. The idea of starting a platform like this originated when I was asked by a couple of friends for information about Windows 8 & Android roms. Back then Windows 8 & JellyBean (Android) had created alot of fuss because of the overhaul in their UI. People were perplexed by the Modern UI of Windows 8 & were skepticle about the performance of the newly refined iteration of Android. Later Qmobile, a Pakistani mobile phone company released a few Android phones, which intrigued my interest in porting & developing JellyBean for the ICS based Qmobiles. On May 6, 2013 I released the stable version of the rom (Android OS) I ported. It was welcomed very warmly & praised by alot of people. A few Pakistani developers used my rom as a base rom for developing custom roms. I was overwhelmed by the popularity of my rom & by the flexibility of Android in terms of modding & porting. Later on I hopped into Android apps & cross-platform game development and have released few successful apps & games. I am also the founder of DocSeek, a health search engine of Pakistan.
